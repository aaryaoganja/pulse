Pulse · Product roadmap

v1 ships now · v2 unlocks after 25-50 customer learning
v1 · SHIPPING

Foundational scope (months 1-6)

What you're seeing in this prototype. 10 capabilities, plus full integration with Superleap's existing CRM, Voice AI, Workflows, Engage, MCP, and AI Labs.
1
8-family scoring framework with cross-family LLM synthesis
Temporal, Leadership, Growth, Distress, Competitive, Fit (filter), Engagement, Network. Two-stage architecture: hard filters then multi-family scoring with stacking, causal chains, and negative-interaction detection.
2
Monday Pulse Brief with prospect prioritization
Karan triages 12 hot prospects in 20 minutes vs. 12-15 hours/week today. Inline Approve/Snooze/Reject actions feed the rejection-reason learning loop.
3
Prospect Dossier with full citations
Every signal cites source, timestamp, confidence, and rubric-match. "View raw evidence" drilldown surfaces the underlying source text.
4
Natural-language rubric (read-only in v1)
Each broker's ICP captured during AI Labs onboarding as plain English. LLM (J5) parses into structured config. Customers view both the original NL description and the parsed structured rubric.
5
On-demand multi-provider contact enrichment
Waterfall across LinkedIn Sales Nav → Apollo → Lusha → India-context provider → Pattern inference. Provider-agnostic. Cost-transparent. Citation-attached. Triggered on click, not in bulk.
6
Outreach generation across Email, WhatsApp, Voice Agent Brief
Three channels, all referencing the specific trigger context. Voice agent brief hands off to Superleap's existing SuperAgents for autonomous qualification calls.
7
Wednesday call-prep brief integrated with SuperAgents
When a meeting is booked, the call brief auto-generates: refreshed trigger, 3 tailored discovery questions, objection-response pairs, competitive context.
8
Friday Pulse Quality Dashboard + feedback loop
Approval rates, false-positive patterns, per-family precision. Rolls into per-broker prompt-tuning loop weekly.
9
Native Lead-pipeline integration
Approved prospects create Superleap Leads with trigger context, evidence, score, and enriched contacts. Every existing Superleap module (Workflows, Engage, MCP) operates on these leads natively.
10
MCP queryability
"Show me Pulse prospects with renewal <60 days" via Claude/ChatGPT/Gemini through Superleap's existing MCP layer.
v2 · ROADMAP

Phase 2 evolution (months 6-18 post-v1)

Deferred deliberately. Each v2 feature unlocks after operational learning from 25-50 v1 customers. Building these before that learning would mean configuring for assumptions we haven't validated.
1
Conversational rubric refinement (J7) v2
Move from read-only to chat-based rubric editing. "Deprioritize pharma for next quarter, boost tech weighting" → LLM proposes diff + simulates impact on current prospect base before activation. Roadmap: months 6-9.
2
Closed-loop pricing intelligence v2
Inferred fair-price benchmark per prospect, based on similar-sized closed deals in broker's own customer base + IRDAI industry data. Triggers at Proposal stage. Roadmap: months 9-12.
3
Multi-policy line cross-sell intelligence v2
Detect when a prospect has triggers across multiple policy lines simultaneously (D&O + group health + cyber). Unified cross-sell brief. Increases ACV per account. Roadmap: months 6-9.
4
Bulk enrichment with budget guardrails v2
Pre-enrich top N likely-approved prospects. Deferred-payment model: cost commits only on approval. Per-broker monthly budget caps with 75%/90% alerts. Roadmap: months 9-12.
5
Self-serve enrichment provider configuration v2
Customers add/remove providers from waterfall, set confidence thresholds, set budget caps per provider, view hit rates. Roadmap: months 6-9.
6
Adjacent vertical templates v2
8-family framework generalizes to corporate banking (MSME lending) and asset management (corporate treasuries). Pre-configured vertical templates. ~3-month pilot per vertical. Roadmap: months 12-18.
7
Federated benchmarking (privacy-preserving) v2
Opt-in shared anonymized signal-conversion patterns across brokers. "Your firm's conversion at 3.1x vs. network average 4.3x." Network effect; brokers improve faster as more join. Roadmap: months 12-18.
8
Outbound sequence orchestration v2
Multi-touch sequences with branch logic. "No reply in 5 days → WhatsApp follow-up referencing trigger updates; no reply in 10 days → voice-agent qualifying call." LLM-orchestrated. Roadmap: months 9-12.
9
Account expansion intelligence v2
For won customers, flip the system: monitor for expansion triggers — new hiring, geography, leadership changes creating upsell opportunity. Roadmap: months 12-18.
10
Multilingual outreach generation v2
Hindi, Tamil, Kannada, Telugu, Marathi, Bengali outreach. Uses Superleap's existing multilingual voice infrastructure. Critical for tier-2/3 broker expansion. Roadmap: months 6-9.
